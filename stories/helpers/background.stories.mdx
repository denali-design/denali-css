# Background
The background helpers include origin, clip, attachement, size, position, and repeat.

---

## Origin

Helper classes for controlling the start of a background image relative to its container.

> For more information on background-origin visit [MDN Web Docs](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/CSS/background-origin).

|Class|Properties|
| ------------- | ---------------- |
|`.bg-origin-padding-box`|background-origin: padding-box;|
|`.bg-origin-border-box`|background-origin: border-box;|
|`.bg-origin-content-box`|background-origin: content-box;|

---

## Clip

> For more information on background-clip visit [MDN Web Docs](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/CSS/background-clip).
